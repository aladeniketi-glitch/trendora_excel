This project involved analyzing customer shopping data for Trendora, a fashion retail brand operating both online and physical stores. The objective was to transform raw transaction data into meaningful business insights by cleaning the dataset, performing data analysis, and developing an interactive Excel dashboard for management reporting.
The dataset was first cleaned to ensure data quality by addressing missing values, correcting formatting inconsistencies, and preparing the data for accurate analysis. Key business metrics were then calculated to provide a quick overview of business performance.
The dashboard includes three Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
•	Total Purchase Amount (USD)
•	Average Customer Review Rating
•	Total Number of Unique Customers
To provide deeper insights into customer purchasing behavior, PivotTables and PivotCharts were created to visualize:
•	Top 5 Most Purchased Items
•	Purchase Distribution by Age Group
•	Purchase Amount by Season
•	Payment Method Breakdown
An interactive Gender Slicer was added to allow users to filter the dashboard dynamically and compare purchasing patterns across male and female customers.
The final dashboard was designed on a single worksheet with a clean, user-friendly layout, enabling stakeholders to quickly monitor sales performance, customer demographics, seasonal spending trends, and payment preferences for informed business decision-making.
